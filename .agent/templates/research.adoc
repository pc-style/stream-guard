=== Trust Level Definitions

* **Verified** — Original source, peer-reviewed, code/artifact reproducible
* **Trusted** — Reputable outlet, known author, corroborated elsewhere
* **Anecdotal** — Forum, blog, interview; single point of failure
* **Suspect** — Conflicts with verified sources, no evidence
* **Debunked** — Contradicted by evidence; included for completeness

=== Sanitation Flags

* **[cite]** — Properly cited, traceable
* **[quote]** — Direct quote, needs context
* **[paraphrase]** — Rewritten, risk of drift
* **[speculation]** — Inference, not evidence
* **[rumor]** — Unsubstantiated
* **[deprecated]** — Outdated, newer info exists
